RATING THE STATEMENTS

PLEASE READ THESE INSTRUCTIONS.

1. Take the cards from the "AGREE"-pile and read them again.

2. Next, place these cards on the grid based on the extent to which you agree with them (+6 being the statement with which you most strongly agree).

3. Now read the cards in the "DISAGREE"-pile again.

4. Just like before, place these cards on the grid based on the extent to which you disagree with them (-6 being the statement with which you most strongly disagree).

5. Finally, read the "NEUTRAL"-cards again and arrange them in the remaining open boxes of the score sheet.

OBS: You are free to change your mind and the placement of the cards.

OBS: The order you put the cards in each column is NOT important.

IF YOUR CARDS BECOME TOO SMALL TO READ: Point the mouse cursor over the card and a tool tip will appear with the full statement.
